Bba

The BBA program at Tribhuvan University provides a robust foundation in business management principles, including finance, marketing, human resources, and strategic management. The BSc program at Tribhuvan University offers a comprehensive education in scientific disciplines such as physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics. It emphasizes critical thinking and practical skills through laboratory work and research, preparing students for careers in science, technology, and related fields.

Bbs

The BBS program at Tribhuvan University provides foundational knowledge in business practices, including accounting, management, and economics. It focuses on developing analytical and managerial skills to prepare students for careers in business and finance sectors.

Bicte

The BICT Education program at Tribhuvan University integrates ICT with educational theory and practice. It prepares students to effectively teach and integrate technology into educational settings, focusing on both technical skills and pedagogical strategies for modern classrooms.

B.ed

The BEd program at Tribhuvan University focuses on preparing future educators with the skills and knowledge needed for effective teaching. It covers educational theories, curriculum development, and classroom management, aiming to equip students for successful careers in education.
